Authentication Bot

Our authentication bot provides groups with a easy and secure way to authenticate their users. It allows users to dm the bot with a choice of a randomly generated key or their email. Full paypal integration is also supported for an extra cost if users bind with their email. Authentication bot data is stored on a google sheet shared with the group owner for easy editing, and all updates to the spreadsheet are transferred to the bot immediatly.

Twitter Monitor

Our Twitter Monitor is the fastest in the game. With .1s response times and tons of success (on @ReachedIO Twitter), you will never be late to a shock drop.

SMS Bot

Our SMS bot allows groups to quickly and easily send out messages to their users during shock drops or to send out important announcements. Users can easily add their numbers through intuitive commands by themselves, and all an admin has to do is write one command to send a message to every user who added their number.

Success Bot

Our success bot allows groups to easily post pictures from their success channel to their twitter without giving the action another thought!

Stocks Bot

Our stocks bot offers realtime data about a company such as stock pricing, last close price, volume, lows, high, percent change, and more, as well as provides both quarterly and annual reports for that company. If a user does not know the ticker for a company, they can simply dm the name of the company and the bot will list possible companies with their tickers and market.

Instagram Monitor

Our instagram monitor checks both posts and stories to detect if a drop has occured, and if it has alert users in the discord. It offers OCR integration as well to make sure you never miss a drop.
